## ‚ú® Park Selector
**Access:** Click "Park Selector" in the navigation or visit `/ai-helper`

### How It Works:
1. **Describe Your Ideal Visit**: Type what you want to do, for example:
   - "I want to run with my dog"
   - "Family day with kids"
   - "Peaceful nature walk"
   - "Sports and recreation"
   - "Picnic and relaxation"

2. **Get Recommendations**: The AI analyzes all 7 Liberty Township parks and recommends the best matches

3. **Review Results**: Each recommendation shows:
   - Match percentage (0-100%)
   - Why it matches (matching activities)
   - Park details and amenities
   - Location information

### Example Searches:
- "I want to run with my dog and have a picnic after" ‚Üí Liberty Park (high match) + Havener Park (alternative)
- "Family outing with kids" ‚Üí Hyatts Park + Liberty Park
- "Nature photography" ‚Üí Havener Park
- "Dog park visit" ‚Üí Big Bear Park

### Pro Tips:
- Be descriptive about what you want to do
- The AI considers:
  - Running/hiking trails
  - Dog-friendly areas
  - Picnic facilities
  - Sports fields
  - Parking availability
  - Accessibility
  - Water features
  - Playgrounds
- Try different search terms to discover parks you didn't know about
- Use recommendations to explore less-crowded parks
